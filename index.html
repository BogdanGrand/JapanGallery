Fujiyama

The highest mountain in Japan is the only natural landmark that everyone, without exception, wants to see and visit. The mountain has a symmetrical cone of volcanic origin. The last time Fuji-san erupted was in 1707, during which a layer of ash covered the streets of Tokyo.
